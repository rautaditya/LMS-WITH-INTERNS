How to accept a course plan?

+

Follow the below steps :-

  1. Login to your vivaaks Account.
  2. Click on "My Subscription" you would see on top of the page.
  3. Click on "1-to-1" Tab under "My Subscription".
  4. You would see your active course plan. Click on "View Details".
  5. You would be able to see a Join Course button. Click on it.
  6. Once you've join the course you would be able to see pay and join option in the right corner.
